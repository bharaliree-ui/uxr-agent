# Data Attribution

The transcripts in this folder are derived from an openly licensed dataset on Figshare.

**Source:** Raulgaonkar, Haresh (2021). *Experience Survey Transcripts.* figshare. Dataset.
https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.14616966.v1

**License:** [CC BY 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/) — free to share and adapt, including commercially, with attribution.

**What these are:** Semi-structured interviews with first-time / first-line managers about their experiences moving into management. Used here as realistic, non-confidential sample data to demonstrate the UX Research Synthesis Agent.

**Changes made:** The original `.docx` files (ESTR1–ESTR5) were converted to plain-text `.txt` for the agent. Speaker labels were normalized ("Me" → "Interviewer"); no interview content was altered. Original `.docx` files are kept alongside for provenance.
